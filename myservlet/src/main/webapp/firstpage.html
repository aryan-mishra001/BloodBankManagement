Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation is a simple act that can save lives. Every two seconds, someone needs blood. Your donation can help accident victims, cancer patients, and those undergoing surgery.

Benefits of Blood Donation

  • Saves multiple lives with one unit of blood.
  • Improves donor’s health by stimulating new blood cell production.
  • Spreads awareness and inspires others to contribute.

Who Can Donate?

  • Be between 18 and 65 years old.
  • Weigh at least 50 kg.
  • Be healthy and free from infections.
  • Have hemoglobin level above 12.5 g/dl.

Donation Process

  1. Registration: Fill out a simple form with your details.
  2. Medical Check: Quick health checkup by a doctor.
  3. Donation: Safe and painless process that takes 10–15 minutes.
  4. Rest & Refreshments: Relax and enjoy light snacks after donation.
